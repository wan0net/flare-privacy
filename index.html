Flare Journal - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Summary

Your data stays on your device and your personal iCloud. The developer has no access to it. Period.

Data Collection

Flare Journal does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No server-side data storage
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No crash reporting services

Data Storage

All data you enter into Flare Journal (health scores, symptoms, lab values, appointments, and notes) is stored exclusively:

  • On your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • In your personal iCloud if you have iCloud enabled for the app (syncing between your own devices only)

The developer cannot access, read, or retrieve your data under any circumstances.

Health Data

Flare Journal processes health-related information (disease activity scores, symptom logs, lab values) entirely on your device. This data is never transmitted to any server. The app does not integrate with Apple HealthKit.

Notifications

Flare Journal can send local notifications to remind you to log your daily scores. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification service.

Data Export

You may choose to export your data (e.g., sharing a report with your doctor). This is entirely at your discretion, and the exported data is shared only with the recipient you choose. The developer is never involved in this process.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, removing the app will also remove the data from iCloud.

Children's Privacy

Flare Journal does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app may be used by minors under the supervision of a parent or guardian for tracking juvenile arthritis or other conditions.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
