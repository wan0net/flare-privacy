Features

Built for your clinical reality

Flare Journal uses the same validated scoring instruments your specialists rely on — so your self-tracking data means something in the consulting room.

📈

Validated Clinical Scores

17 validated scores across 14 conditions — CDAI, HBI, BASDAI, DAS28-CRP, RAPID3, and more. The same instruments your specialists use, computed from your daily logs.

📊

Longitudinal Charting

Visualise your disease activity over weeks and months with severity-zone charts. See patterns, track treatment response, spot flares early.

🔒

Privacy First

Zero servers. Zero accounts. Zero tracking. All data stays on your device. The developer cannot access your data.

🛡️

Your data is yours. Always.

Health data is among the most sensitive personal information that exists. Flare Journal is built on a simple principle: the developer has no access to your data.

There are no user accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no data ever leaves your device. Everything is stored locally — encrypted, private, and under your control.
